FAQ

Questions, answered

Everything you might want to know before you join.

Is my team's data secure?
Yes. Trulu encrypts all data in transit and at rest (AES-256), uses role-based access with full audit logs, and is built to HIPAA and SOC 2 standards — important for healthcare teams handling sensitive records.
Can I migrate from my current HR or payroll tool?
Absolutely. Import your team via spreadsheet or a direct connection, and Trulu's AI will parse existing documents (offer letters, W-4s, licenses) to fill in profiles automatically. Most teams are set up in under a day.
When does Trulu launch?
We're in pre-launch now. Join the waitlist to get early access, lock in founding-member pricing, and help shape the roadmap. We'll email you the moment your workspace is ready.
How does the AI actually help?
Trulu AI reads across your whole workspace: it gives you a daily brief of what needs attention, scans every payroll run for anomalies, parses uploaded documents, drafts performance reviews, and answers plain-English questions about your team and pay.
What does it cost?
Plans and founding-member pricing will be shared with waitlist members first. Pre-launch teams start free — join the waitlist to lock in early-access perks.
Do you handle taxes and filings?
Yes — Trulu calculates, withholds, and files federal, state, and local payroll taxes automatically, for both employees and contractors, so nothing slips through the cracks.
